What is the differences between text mirroring and upside-down text inversion formats?

Text mirroring reverses the sequence of letters from right to left (e.g., "abc" becomes "cba"). Upside-down text uses a mathematical character mapping array to replace letters with upside-down and backwards symbols (e.g., "abc" becomes "ɔqɐ"), flipping the entire line vertically.
