6. Common Meme Making Mistakes to Avoid

  • Over-Scaling Long Phrases: Typing massive sentences while keeping maximum font sizes will force text past the outer edges of your template. Adjust the font size slider downwards to fit long jokes properly.
  • Obscuring Key Character Faces: Placing captions directly over main subject faces ruins the joke's impact. Use our drag feature to reposition text into blank negative spaces.
  • Using Sluggish Server-Side Compilers: Uploading heavy background templates to slow remote sites wastes data packages and adds processing delays. Stick to browser-native canvas options.
