6. Common Text Overlay Mistakes to Avoid

  • Ignoring Background Contrast: Writing dark text over shadow areas or white captions on sky elements makes labels unreadable. Use highly contrasting colors to ensure legibility.
  • Over-Scaling Font Sizes: Selecting maximum text pixel scale dimensions on small photos will cause words to overflow past the image border lines. Check your layout preview carefully.
  • Sending Private Materials to Cloud Portals: Uploading private identification scans or proprietary company images to third-party remote sites poses serious data leak risks.
